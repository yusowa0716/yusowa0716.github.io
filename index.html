Yusong Wang

I received my B.S. in the Special Class for the Gifted Young (SCGY) program at Xi'an Jiaotong University, majoring in Automation. Currently, I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate jointly trained at Microsoft Research Asia and Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics in Xi'an Jiaotong University, under the supervision of Dr. Bin Shao and Academician of Chinese Academy of Engineering, Dr. Nanning Zheng.

I served as a research intern at Microsoft Research AI4Science supervised by Dr. Bin Shao from July 2020.

Research Interests

I'm primarily interested in AI4Science, particularly in machine learning force fields. Recently, I've developed a strong interest in Generative AI as well. I'm dedicated to using generative models to explore new solutions in science, including potential energy surface sampling, protein-ligand docking, etc. Representative papers are highlighted.
